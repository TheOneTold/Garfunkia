Welcome to the Web site for the Association for Responsible Web Design.

The goal of the A.R.W.D. is to promote safe, user-friendly websites. The A.R.W.D. was created in 2024 as a response to the large number of sites on the Internet today that feature: intrusitve scripts, inappropriate tracking, obnoxious advertising, and overall convoluted designs. The A.R.W.D. seeks to promote and reward Web sites that follow responsible Web design principles.

The A.R.W.D. awards stars to Web sites that we have certified as following our responsibility standards. Rankings range from Tin tier to Gold tier depending on how well the website conforms to the standards we have laid out. Web sites that have been deemed unsafe by the A.R.W.D. are given the condemned tier to denote that they should be avoided. Click the "Awards" button below to view our ranking system. Click on the "Index" button below to view an index of Web sites that have been rewarded stars by the A.R.W.D.
