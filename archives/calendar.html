Garfunkian Calendar

The Garfunkian leap year is tied to the Gregorian leap year. This means that the Garfunkian leap years occur every four years starting from GY1. GY1 was the first leap year, and the leap year after that was GY5. A leap year is skipped every hundred years starting in GY81, although GY381 and every other odd century following that remain leap years.
